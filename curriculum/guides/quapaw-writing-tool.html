Write & Copy Text in Quapaw

We created the tool below to make it easy for those creating short-form translations or writing in Okáxpa. Clicking the text box below will open a virtual keyboard containing all the characters used in the Quapaw language. Use the keyboard to type out your message then click the check mark at the bottom-right portion of the keyboard to close it.

Click the Copy Text button below the field to copy the contents to your device's clipboard.
