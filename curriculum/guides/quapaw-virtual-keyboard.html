Overview

This tool provides an easy method to write text in the Quapaw language. Our department created this tool to streamline our translations and writing in Okáxpa, but we think it should be available to all who need it.

Click the text field below to open the virtual keyboard. It contains all the characters and accent marks used in the Quapaw language. Type out your message then click the check mark button to close it.

Click the Copy Text button below the field to copy the contents to your device's clipboard; click the Clear Field button to empty the field of its content.
